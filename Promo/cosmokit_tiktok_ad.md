# CosmoKit TikTok Ad

## Objective

Sell CosmoKit to iOS developers with a fast, native-looking TikTok ad that frames the app as the control center for Simulator workflows.

## Format

- Aspect ratio: `9:16`
- Resolution: `1080x1920`
- Length: `18-20s`
- Language: `English`
- Delivery: mute-first, readable with burned-in copy

## Creative Angle

- Open with friction: most iOS devs still test scattered flows with terminal commands, hidden debug menus, and repetitive setup.
- Reframe CosmoKit as the single cockpit for simulator QA, capture, and debugging.
- Keep every section benefit-led. Do not list features without a testing outcome.
- Make the edit feel like native product content, not a glossy enterprise ad.

## Edit Structure

1. Hook
   `Still testing iOS flows the hard way?`
   `Your Simulator needs a cockpit`
2. Speed
   `Pushes, deep links, and mocked location`
   `Trigger hidden flows in seconds`
3. QA
   `Permissions, biometrics, resets`
   `Repeat edge cases without setup pain`
4. Capture
   `Screenshots and video for QA and App Store`
   `Ship proof, not explanations`
5. Pro
   `Proxy, response overrides, multi-simulator`
   `For heavier debugging sessions`
6. CTA
   `CosmoKit`
   `Free tools now. Pro workflows when you need them`

## Shot Plan

1. `0.0s - 2.8s`
   Use the main product demo with the strongest visual motion.
2. `2.8s - 5.8s`
   Show deep links, push notifications, or location actions.
3. `5.8s - 8.8s`
   Show QA toolkit interactions and repeatable state changes.
4. `8.8s - 11.8s`
   Show screenshot or video capture tooling.
5. `11.8s - 14.8s`
   Show more advanced screens for proxy, overrides, or multiple simulators.
6. `14.8s - 18.6s`
   End card with icon, product name, and App Store CTA.

## Caption Options

- Stop juggling `simctl`, debug menus, and manual setup. CosmoKit turns Simulator work into one fast control center for iOS devs.
- QA, deep links, pushes, mocked location, screenshots, video, proxy, overrides. One app. One Simulator cockpit.
- Build faster. Test sharper. Capture everything.

## On-Screen CTA

`Download CosmoKit on the Mac App Store`
